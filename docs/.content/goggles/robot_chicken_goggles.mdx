## Overview

The Robot Chicken Goggles provide advanced night vision capabilities combined with a unique ore mining reward system. When active, mining ore blocks has a chance to generate Cyber Chicken Eggs, providing an extra incentive for mining operations.

## Features

### Base Functionality
- **Night Vision**: Provides clear vision in complete darkness
- **Multiple Modes**: `ON / OFF / AUTO` switching
- **Auto Mode**: Automatically activates below light level 7
- **Energy Consumption**: 1 energy per second while active
- **Maximum Charge**: 1800 energy units
- **Rechargeable**: Uses Spider Eyes, Fermented Spider Eyes, or Glowstone Dust
- **Versatile Equipment**: Can be worn as helmet or in curios/trinket slot

### Enhanced Features
- **+2 Armor Protection**: Improved defense compared to base goggles
- **Cyber Chicken Egg Generation**: Chance to spawn eggs when mining ores

## Cyber Chicken Egg System

<Callout variant="warning">
The Robot Chicken Goggles must be active when mining ore blocks for the egg generation to trigger. Keep the goggles charged during mining sessions!
</Callout>

### How It Works
- **Trigger**: Mining any ore block while goggles are active
- **Chance-based**: Random probability to generate an egg
- **Automatic**: Eggs appear in your inventory automatically
- **No Extra Cost**: No additional energy consumed for egg generation

### Compatible Ores
The system works with blocks tagged as ores, including:
- All vanilla ores (iron, gold, diamond, coal, redstone, lapis, emerald, copper)
- Nether ores (nether gold, nether quartz, ancient debris)
- Modded ores (if properly tagged)
- Deepslate variants
- Raw ore blocks

### Cyber Chicken Eggs
- **Item**: `calypsos_nightvision_goggles:cyber_chicken_egg`
- **Use**: Robot Chicken mod integration
- **Collection**: Can be stored for later use
- **Value**: Varies based on Robot Chicken mod features

## Crafting

<PrefabObtaining />

The Robot Chicken Goggles are crafted by upgrading the base <ContentLink id="calypsos_nightvision_goggles:nightvision_goggles">Night Vision Goggles</ContentLink> with additional materials.

## Usage

### Equipping
- Place in helmet armor slot for protection and egg generation
- Alternatively, equip in curios/trinket slot if you have compatible mods installed
- Right-click while equipped to cycle through modes

### Mode Switching
- **ON**: Continuously active night vision and egg generation
- **OFF**: Completely disabled, conserves energy
- **AUTO**: Automatically activates in darkness (light level < 7)

### Mining for Eggs
1. Activate the goggles (ON or AUTO mode)
2. Mine ore blocks as normal
3. Cyber Chicken Eggs will occasionally appear in your inventory
4. Continue mining for more chances

### Energy Management
- Monitor energy level through the HUD display
- Egg generation doesn't consume extra energy
- Recharge using:
  - <Asset location="minecraft:spider_eye" width={16} height={16} /> Spider Eyes
  - <Asset location="minecraft:fermented_spider_eye" width={16} height={16} /> Fermented Spider Eyes
  - <Asset location="minecraft:glowstone_dust" width={16} height={16} /> Glowstone Dust

## Tips & Strategies

<Callout variant="default" title="Pro Tip">
Use AUTO mode during cave mining to conserve energy while still benefiting from egg generation in dark areas where ores are most common.
</Callout>

- **Branch Mining**: Perfect for systematic ore collection in caves
- **Fortune Enchantment**: Combine with Fortune pickaxe for maximum ore yield
- **Energy Conservation**: AUTO mode works well since most ores are found in darkness
- **Ore Hunting**: Prioritize ore-rich areas like caves, ravines, and deep underground
- **Egg Collection**: Save up eggs for Robot Chicken mod features
- **Multiplayer**: Great for mining sessions where extra rewards are appreciated

## Compatibility

<Callout variant="default" title="Robot Chicken Mod Integration">
This feature works best with the **Robot Chicken** mod installed, which provides uses for the Cyber Chicken Eggs. Without it, eggs can still be collected but may have limited use.
</Callout>

- **Robot Chicken Mod**: Full integration for egg utility
- **Without Robot Chicken**: Eggs still generate but may lack functionality
- **Ore Tags**: Works with any block tagged as an ore
