# Cute Chicken Goggles

<Callout variant="info" title="Upgrade Item">
The Cute Chicken Goggles is an upgraded version of the <ContentLink id="calypsos_nightvision_goggles:nightvision_goggles">Night Vision Goggles</ContentLink>, retaining all base functionality with additional chicken-themed enhancements.
</Callout>

> "Embrace the chicken within!"

## Overview

The Cute Chicken Goggles provide advanced night vision capabilities combined with adorable chicken-themed abilities. These goggles reduce fall damage, automatically lay eggs in your inventory, and attract nearby chickens to follow you, making them perfect for chicken farmers and parkour enthusiasts alike.

## Features

### Base Functionality
- **Night Vision**: Provides clear vision in complete darkness
- **Multiple Modes**: `ON / OFF / AUTO` switching
- **Auto Mode**: Automatically activates below light level 7
- **Energy Consumption**: 1 energy per second while active
- **Maximum Charge**: 1800 energy units
- **Rechargeable**: Uses Spider Eyes, Fermented Spider Eyes, or Glowstone Dust
- **Versatile Equipment**: Can be worn as helmet or in curios/trinket slot

### Enhanced Features
- **+2 Armor Protection**: Improved defense compared to base goggles
- **Fall Damage Reduction**: Reduced damage from falls while active
- **Daily Egg Production**: Automatically lays one egg per day
- **Chicken Attraction**: Nearby chickens follow you

## Special Abilities

### Fall Damage Reduction

<Callout variant="warning">
The goggles must be **active** for fall damage reduction to work. Falls while goggles are disabled or uncharged will deal full damage.
</Callout>

- **Effect**: Significantly reduced fall damage
- **Activation**: Only when goggles are active (ON or AUTO mode in darkness)
- **Stacking**: Can combine with other fall damage reduction (Feather Falling, Slow Falling, etc.)
- **No Extra Cost**: Doesn't consume additional energy

### Daily Egg Production

One of the most unique features - passive egg generation:

<Callout variant="default" title="Passive Income">
The goggles automatically produce one egg per real-world day at a random time. No activation required!
</Callout>

- **Frequency**: Once per real-world day
- **Timing**: Random time each day
- **Automatic**: Appears in inventory automatically
- **Item**: Regular Minecraft egg
- **Always Active**: Works even when goggles are not equipped or activated

### Chicken Attraction

When equipped, chickens in the area will follow you:

- **Range**: Nearby chickens (similar to holding seeds)
- **Effect**: Chickens follow and gather around you
- **Breeding**: Useful for leading chickens to breeding pens
- **Always Active**: Works while equipped, regardless of activation state

## Crafting

<PrefabObtaining />

The Cute Chicken Goggles are crafted by upgrading the base <ContentLink id="calypsos_nightvision_goggles:nightvision_goggles">Night Vision Goggles</ContentLink> with additional materials.

## Usage

### Equipping
- Place in helmet armor slot for fall damage reduction and chicken attraction
- Alternatively, equip in curios/trinket slot if you have compatible mods installed
- Right-click while equipped to cycle through modes

### Mode Switching
- **ON**: Continuously active night vision and fall damage reduction
- **OFF**: Only chicken attraction works; no night vision or fall protection
- **AUTO**: Automatically activates in darkness (light level < 7) with all benefits

### Fall Damage Reduction
- Activate the goggles before dangerous jumps or falls
- Combines well with water bucket clutches and parkour
- Especially useful in cave exploration and mountain climbing

### Chicken Management
- Simply equip the goggles to attract chickens
- Lead chickens to breeding pens or new locations
- Useful for chicken farming operations
- Works even when goggles are not activated

### Egg Collection
- Check your inventory periodically for the daily egg
- Eggs appear at random times throughout the day
- Stackable with other eggs for storage

### Energy Management
- Monitor energy level through the HUD display
- Fall damage reduction and chicken attraction don't consume extra energy
- Recharge using:
  - <Asset location="minecraft:spider_eye" width={16} height={16} /> Spider Eyes
  - <Asset location="minecraft:fermented_spider_eye" width={16} height={16} /> Fermented Spider Eyes
  - <Asset location="minecraft:glowstone_dust" width={16} height={16} /> Glowstone Dust

## Tips & Strategies

<Callout variant="default" title="Pro Tip">
Perfect for skyblock and vertical base designs! The fall damage reduction makes navigating tall structures much safer.
</Callout>

- **Parkour Practice**: Reduced fall damage means less risk during practice
- **Cave Exploration**: Survive unexpected drops in ravines and caves
- **Building Projects**: Safer when working on tall structures
- **Chicken Farming**: Easy chicken management with the attraction feature
- **Daily Routine**: Check inventory once daily for the free egg
- **MLG Backup**: Provides a safety net if water bucket clutches fail
- **Elytra Alternative**: Useful before obtaining elytra wings
