# The Watcher Goggles

<Callout variant="info" title="Upgrade Item">
The Watcher Goggles is an upgraded version of the <ContentLink id="calypsos_nightvision_goggles:nightvision_goggles">Night Vision Goggles</ContentLink>, retaining all base functionality with additional enhancements.
</Callout>

> "Wait... what if this timeline's future isn't the one we expected?"

## Overview

The Watcher Goggles provide advanced night vision capabilities combined with a unique eye contact debuff system. When active, any entity that makes eye contact with the wearer will be afflicted with the **"It's Watching!"** debuff, weakening their combat abilities and mobility.

## Features

### Base Functionality
- **Night Vision**: Provides clear vision in complete darkness
- **Multiple Modes**: `ON / OFF / AUTO` switching
- **Auto Mode**: Automatically activates below light level 7
- **Energy Consumption**: 1 energy per second while active
- **Maximum Charge**: 1800 energy units
- **Rechargeable**: Uses Spider Eyes, Fermented Spider Eyes, or Glowstone Dust
- **Versatile Equipment**: Can be worn as helmet or in curios/trinket slot

### Enhanced Features
- **+2 Armor Protection**: Improved defense compared to base goggles
- **It's Watching! Debuff**: Weakens entities that make eye contact

## It's Watching! Effect

When the Watcher Goggles are active, entities that meet your gaze suffer the **"It's Watching!"** debuff:

<Callout variant="warning">
The effect triggers when entities look directly at you while the goggles are active. The debuff significantly reduces their combat effectiveness.
</Callout>

### Debuff Effects
- **Target Illumination**: Affected entities are highlighted
- **Distorted Audio**: Eerie sounds emitted from affected targets
- **Reduced Aggression**: Attack damage decreased by 4 points
- **Reduced Mobility**: Movement speed reduced by 15%
- **Behavioral State**: **Submissive** - enemies become less threatening

## Crafting

<PrefabObtaining />

The Watcher Goggles are crafted by upgrading the base <ContentLink id="calypsos_nightvision_goggles:nightvision_goggles">Night Vision Goggles</ContentLink> with additional materials.

## Usage

### Equipping
- Place in helmet armor slot for protection and the watching effect
- Alternatively, equip in curios/trinket slot if you have compatible mods installed
- Right-click while equipped to cycle through modes

### Mode Switching
- **ON**: Continuously active night vision and watching effect
- **OFF**: Completely disabled, conserves energy
- **AUTO**: Automatically activates in darkness (light level < 7)

### Triggering the Effect
- Simply face entities while the goggles are active
- Works on all living entities (hostile and neutral mobs)
- Effect applies automatically when they look at you
- No additional energy cost for the debuff

### Energy Management
- Monitor energy level through the HUD display
- Recharge using:
  - <Asset location="minecraft:spider_eye" width={16} height={16} /> Spider Eyes
  - <Asset location="minecraft:fermented_spider_eye" width={16} height={16} /> Fermented Spider Eyes
  - <Asset location="minecraft:glowstone_dust" width={16} height={16} /> Glowstone Dust

## Tips & Strategies

<Callout variant="default" title="Pro Tip">
The Watcher Goggles excel in combat situations where you're outnumbered. The debuff makes it easier to survive encounters with multiple enemies.
</Callout>

- **Crowd Control**: Perfect for handling multiple enemies at once
- **Boss Preparation**: Weaken powerful mobs before engaging in melee combat
- **Dungeon Raids**: Makes navigating spawner rooms and fortresses safer
- **PvP Advantage**: Forces opponents to fight with reduced damage and speed
- **Exploration**: Reduces threat from hostile mobs during nighttime exploration
- **No Extra Cost**: The watching effect doesn't consume additional energy
