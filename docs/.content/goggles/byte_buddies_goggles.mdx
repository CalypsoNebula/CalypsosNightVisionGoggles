<Callout variant="info" title="Crossover Item">
The ByteBuddies Goggles is an upgraded version of the <ContentLink id="calypsos_nightvision_goggles:nightvision_goggles">Night Vision Goggles</ContentLink> with special integration for the ByteBuddies mod.

## Overview

The ByteBuddies Goggles provide advanced night vision capabilities combined with mining enhancement and ByteBuddy support features. These goggles consume energy to boost mining speed and provide free movement speed buffs to nearby ByteBuddy entities, making them perfect for collaborative mining operations.

## Features

### Base Functionality
- **Night Vision**: Provides clear vision in complete darkness
- **Multiple Modes**: `ON / OFF / AUTO` switching
- **Auto Mode**: Automatically activates below light level 7
- **Energy Consumption**: 1 energy per second while active
- **Maximum Charge**: 1800 energy units
- **Rechargeable**: Uses Spider Eyes, Fermented Spider Eyes, or Glowstone Dust
- **Versatile Equipment**: Can be worn as helmet or in curios/trinket slot

### Enhanced Features
- **+2 Armor Protection**: Improved defense compared to base goggles
- **Mining Speed Boost**: Consumes energy to mine faster while breaking blocks
- **ByteBuddy Support**: Grants free Speed II buff to nearby ByteBuddies

## Special Abilities

### Mining Enhancement

When active and breaking blocks, the ByteBuddies Goggles provide a significant mining speed boost:

<Callout variant="warning">
The mining enhancement consumes additional energy with each block broken. Make sure to keep the goggles well-charged during mining sessions.
</Callout>

- **Effect**: Increased mining/digging speed while active
- **Energy Cost**: Additional energy per block broken
- **Works on**: All breakable blocks
- **Activation**: Automatic when breaking blocks

### ByteBuddy Speed Boost

<Callout variant="default" title="ByteBuddies Mod Integration">
This feature requires the **ByteBuddies** mod to be installed. Without it, only the night vision and mining boost will function.
</Callout>

- **Range**: 30 block radius around the wearer
- **Effect**: Speed II (Movement Speed +40%) for 60 seconds
- **Target**: All ByteBuddy entities within range
- **Cost**: Free - no additional energy consumption
- **Cooldown**: 3 seconds between applications per ByteBuddy

## Crafting

<PrefabObtaining />

The ByteBuddies Goggles are crafted by upgrading the base <ContentLink id="calypsos_nightvision_goggles:nightvision_goggles">Night Vision Goggles</ContentLink> with additional materials.

## Usage

### Equipping
- Place in helmet armor slot for protection and mining boost
- Alternatively, equip in curios/trinket slot if you have compatible mods installed
- Right-click while equipped to cycle through modes

### Mode Switching
- **ON**: Continuously active night vision, mining boost, and ByteBuddy support
- **OFF**: Completely disabled, conserves energy
- **AUTO**: Automatically activates in darkness (light level < 7)

### Mining Enhancement
- Simply break blocks while the goggles are active
- Mining speed boost applies automatically
- Monitor energy consumption during extended mining sessions

### ByteBuddy Support
- Keep goggles active near ByteBuddy entities
- Speed boost applies automatically every 3 seconds
- No extra energy cost for this feature

### Energy Management
- Monitor energy level through the HUD display
- Higher energy consumption during active mining
- Recharge using:
  - <Asset location="minecraft:spider_eye" width={16} height={16} /> Spider Eyes
  - <Asset location="minecraft:fermented_spider_eye" width={16} height={16} /> Fermented Spider Eyes
  - <Asset location="minecraft:glowstone_dust" width={16} height={16} /> Glowstone Dust

## Tips & Strategies

<Callout variant="default" title="Pro Tip">
Use AUTO mode when mining to conserve energy - the goggles will only activate in dark caves where you need the mining boost most.
</Callout>

- **Strip Mining**: Perfect for branch mining operations in caves
- **Quarrying**: Speeds up large-scale excavation projects
- **ByteBuddy Teams**: Coordinate with ByteBuddies for efficient resource gathering
- **Night Mining**: Combine night vision with mining speed for surface mining at night
- **Energy Planning**: Bring extra recharge materials for extended mining sessions
- **Multiplayer**: The ByteBuddy speed boost makes team mining more efficient

## Compatibility

- **ByteBuddies Mod**: Full integration with ByteBuddy speed buffs
- **Without ByteBuddies**: Mining boost and night vision still function normally
